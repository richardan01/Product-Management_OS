# Weekly Update

**Agent:** Stakeholder Manager — see `Agents/stakeholder-manager/AGENT.md` for full context.
**Sub-agents:** Spawn `task-analyzer` and `metrics-reader` in parallel to gather task progress and latest metrics.

Read the following files to draft Richard's weekly update for Jervis and the Digital Growth team:
1. `Tasks/active.md` — what was completed and what's in progress
2. `GOALS.md` — check against 30-60-90 day targets
3. `Projects/cdp-implementation/brief.md` — milestone status
4. `Meetings/1on1s/jervis.md` (if exists) — any context from last 1:1
5. Check `Meetings/` folder for any relevant meeting notes from this week

Then draft a weekly update using `Templates/weekly-update.md`. Keep it:
- Under 300 words
- Concise and scannable — Jervis is busy
- Honest about blockers — don't bury risks
- Focused on what matters to Jervis: progress toward goals, blockers that need him, decisions needed

Status assessment:
- 🟢 On Track — milestones are progressing as planned
- 🟡 At Risk — a dependency or blocker could affect the timeline
- 🔴 Blocked — cannot progress without external input

After drafting, show it to Richard and ask:
- "Does this accurately reflect your week?"
- "Any metrics or results to add?"
- "Any decisions you need from Jervis to call out?"

Revise based on feedback.

Also pull latest metrics from `Knowledge/Reference/metrics/latest.md` if it exists, and include key numbers (ROAS, activation, retention) in the update.
