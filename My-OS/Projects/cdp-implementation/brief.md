## The Opportunity

### Problem Statement

The Digital Growth team at Kpay operates with siloed customer data across multiple tools — paid ads platforms, email/lifecycle, website analytics, and transaction systems. There is no single view of the customer, making it impossible to:

- Run coordinated, data-driven lifecycle campaigns
- Build accurate first-party audiences for paid ads
- Understand what drives merchant activation and retention
- Personalize marketing messages by segment

As a result, marketing is less efficient (higher CAC, lower ROAS), retention efforts are reactive rather than proactive, and the team makes decisions based on incomplete data.

- Team members (Rachel, Xinyi, Lina, Mardiana) lack unified customer data to do their jobs effectively
- No single source of truth for customer segments across channels

### Strategic Fit

A CDP is the foundational infrastructure for Kpay's Digital Growth ambitions. It enables:
- Retention campaigns (Rachel)
- Paid ads efficiency (Xinyi)
- Web personalization (Lina)
- Content targeting (Mardiana)
- Future: AI-driven segmentation, loyalty, attribution

## Proposed Solution

### High-Level Approach

1. **Identify flight 1 use case** — highest impact, lowest complexity use case to prove CDP value quickly
2. **Select CDP vendor** — evaluate 2–3 options against Kpay's use case and data stack
3. **Implement MVP** — connect core data sources, build flight 1 use case
4. **Drive adoption** — onboard 2+ team members, show measurable results
5. **Expand** — build out remaining use cases on the roadmap

### Key Outcomes

- Unified customer profile across transactions, web, email, paid ads
- At least 1 CDP-powered use case live and showing business results
- Digital Growth team self-sufficient in running segments and activating data

## Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|--------|-----------|
| No data engineering resource available | Medium | High | Identify eng partner early; consider no-code CDP options |
| Budget not approved | Low | High | Build clear ROI case; start with lower-cost options |
| Data quality issues slow implementation | Medium | Medium | Audit data quality in discovery phase |
| Low team adoption | Low | High | Involve team in use case selection; show value fast |
| Scope creep to "boil the ocean" | Medium | Medium | Lock flight 1 scope tightly; park everything else |
