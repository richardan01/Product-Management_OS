# User Research Synthesis Workflow

This workflow synthesizes insights from user interviews into actionable findings for the product team.

## When to Trigger

Say "synthesize interviews" or "research synthesis" after completing a batch of user interviews (typically 5-8 interviews on a specific topic).

## What This Produces

A synthesis document that includes:
- Key themes and patterns across interviews
- Supporting quotes and evidence
- Prioritized opportunities
- Recommended next steps

## Key Constraints

- Never cherry-pick quotes that only support a pre-existing hypothesis
- Include disconfirming evidence and edge cases
- Distinguish between "1 person said" vs "most participants said"
- Tie findings to specific product decisions when possible

## Files in This Workflow

1. `workflow-spec.md` - Full process overview
2. `interview-review.md` - Step 1: How to review each interview
3. `pattern-extraction.md` - Step 2: Identifying patterns across interviews
4. `synthesis-template.md` - Step 3: Final output format
5. `examples/` - Past synthesis documents for reference

## Input Required

- Interview transcripts or detailed notes in `/Research/interviews/`
- Research question or hypothesis being explored
- Context on what product decisions this will inform

## Typical Runtime

~45 minutes for 6 interviews with Claude assistance (vs 3-4 hours manually).
