# Weekly Stakeholder Update Workflow

This workflow produces my Friday stakeholder email summarizing product progress, metrics, and upcoming priorities.

## When to Trigger

Say "stakeholder update" or "Friday update" to run this workflow.

## What This Produces

A single email that goes to leadership (CEO, CTO, VP Product) every Friday by 4pm PT. The email covers:
- Key metrics movement
- Shipped features and progress
- Blockers and risks
- Next week priorities

## Key Constraints

- Keep total length under 500 words - leadership skims these
- Lead with metrics, not activity
- Flag blockers early and with proposed solutions
- Reference OKRs when showing progress

## Files in This Workflow

1. `workflow-spec.md` - Full process overview
2. `gather-metrics.md` - Where to pull metrics and what to include
3. `draft-template.md` - Email structure and example sections
4. `stakeholder-preferences.md` - Who wants what level of detail
5. `past-updates/` - Previous updates for tone and format reference

## Typical Runtime

~15 minutes with Claude assistance (vs 45 min manually).
