# Examples

These examples are sanitized demo workspaces.

They show what a filled ProductManagement-OS can look like after onboarding and a few days of usage, without exposing private company data.

## Why these exist
- Help new users understand expected output quality quickly.
- Show practical usage patterns for AI Product Management work.
- Provide portfolio-friendly artifacts for public review.

## Privacy guidance
Do not store private company context, confidential strategy, or personal notes in public repos. Keep real operating files private and publish only sanitized examples.

## Sanitization rule for new demos
Before committing a new file under `examples/`, confirm:
- No real stakeholder names, manager names, or org charts
- No live company metrics, internal product names, or unreleased strategy
- No real credentials, internal URLs, ticket IDs, or meeting notes
- Replace company names with generic descriptors (e.g. "a B2B SaaS company")
- The artifact still reads as a realistic PM workflow — sanitization should not hollow it out
