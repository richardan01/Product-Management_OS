# Project Brief: CDP Implementation

## One-liner

Implement a Customer Data Platform to give KPay a single source of truth for event tracking and customer lifecycle tagging/tracking.

---

## Problem

Today, KPay's customer data is fragmented across multiple tools with no single source of truth. This creates several compounding problems:

- **No unified customer profile** — the same customer may exist differently across CRM, email, analytics, and ad platforms with no reconciliation
- **Unreliable attribution** — it's unclear which channels or touchpoints drive conversions because events aren't consistently tracked across web, mobile, and email
- **Manual segmentation** — building audiences requires engineering or data team involvement; marketing can't self-serve
- **No lifecycle visibility** — customer lifecycle stages (e.g. onboarding, active, at-risk, churned) are not consistently defined or tracked in real time
- **Data activations are slow** — time from insight to campaign action is high because data isn't centralised or actionable

## Goal

A unified CDP that:
- Tracks all relevant customer events consistently across surfaces
- Tags customers by lifecycle stage in real time
- Enables downstream teams (marketing, product, growth) to act on reliable data
