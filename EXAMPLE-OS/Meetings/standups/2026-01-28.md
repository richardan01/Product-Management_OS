## Discussion

**Grade override edge case (James):**
When a grade is overridden manually, do we show both the original calculated grade and the override in history? Or just the final?

Discussion got into the weeds. Three options:
1. Show only final grade (simplest)
2. Show both with "override" label (more transparent)
3. Show full audit trail (most complex)

Decision: Option 2 for MVP. TAs want to see when they overrode, that's the main use case. Full audit trail is V2.
