## Key Commands

### `/standup`
Morning briefing. Claude will:
- Summarize what's on my plate today
- Surface any blockers or upcoming deadlines
- Remind me of meetings and their context
- Suggest priorities based on my goals

### `/weekly-update`
End-of-week reflection and planning. Claude will:
- Review what shipped this week
- Update progress against Q1 goals
- Flag risks or slipping timelines
- Draft my weekly status update for leadership

### `/synthesize-research`
When I've dumped raw notes, Claude will:
- Extract key insights and patterns
- Connect findings to my active projects
- Identify gaps in my understanding
- Suggest next research questions

## How to Work With Me

**When I ask about a project:** Check `Projects/` first for the latest spec and decision log.

**When I'm preparing for a meeting:** Look in `Meetings/` for past notes with that person/group.

**When I need to make a decision:** Reference `GOALS.md` for my priorities and success metrics.

**When I'm stuck:** Ask me clarifying questions. I value being challenged.
