## Writing Tips

### Executive Summary
- Write this last, after you've completed the analysis
- Each bullet should stand alone
- Include the "so what" - implications, not just observations

### Findings
- Lead with the insight, not the data
- "Teachers struggle to provide timely feedback" not "4 of 6 teachers mentioned feedback timing"
- Include the strongest quotes that capture the essence

### Opportunities
- Frame as "How might we..." or potential directions
- Connect directly to findings
- Estimate impact where possible

### Recommendations
- Be specific and actionable
- Include enough context for someone to act on it
- Distinguish between "do now" and "explore later"

## Sharing the Synthesis

1. **Async first**: Share doc with stakeholders 24 hours before discussion
2. **Highlight key decisions**: Call out what choices this enables
3. **Invite questions**: Create space for clarifying questions
4. **Document outcomes**: After discussion, note what decisions were made
