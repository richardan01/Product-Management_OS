# Weekly Stakeholder Update - Workflow Spec

## Purpose

Deliver a consistent, high-signal Friday update that keeps leadership informed without requiring sync meetings.

## Process Overview

```
Step 1: Gather Metrics (5 min)
    ↓
Step 2: Draft Update (8 min)
    ↓
Step 3: Review & Adjust (2 min)
    ↓
Step 4: Send via Gmail
```

## Step 1: Gather Metrics

See `gather-metrics.md` for full details.

**Quick checklist:**
- [ ] Pull Amplitude dashboard for Teacher Activation funnel
- [ ] Check Stripe for MRR and trial conversions
- [ ] Get support ticket count from Intercom
- [ ] Note any significant product events (launches, incidents)

## Step 2: Draft Update

See `draft-template.md` for the email structure.

**Key sections:**
1. TL;DR (2-3 bullets max)
2. Metrics snapshot
3. What shipped
4. Blockers/risks
5. Next week focus

## Step 3: Review & Adjust

Before sending:
- Verify all numbers are current
- Check tone (confident but honest about challenges)
- Ensure blockers have proposed next steps
- Confirm nothing is confidential that shouldn't be shared

## Step 4: Send

Recipients: sarah@gradeflow.io, james@gradeflow.io, priya@gradeflow.io
CC: product-team@gradeflow.io
Subject line format: `Product Update - Week of [Date]`

## Failure Modes to Avoid

| Failure | Prevention |
|---------|------------|
| Missing metrics | Pull from dashboards first, before writing |
| Too long | Enforce 500 word limit, use bullets |
| Burying bad news | Put blockers in dedicated section, not footnotes |
| Vague priorities | Tie next week focus to specific OKR outcomes |

## Artifacts Produced

- Email sent to stakeholders
- Copy saved in `past-updates/` for future reference
