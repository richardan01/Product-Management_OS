## Example: Filled Template

```markdown
## Q1 2026 Product OKRs - GradeFlow

### O1: Make every teacher successful in their first week
**Why:** Activation is our #1 growth lever. Teachers who activate retain 3x better.

- KR1: Teacher Activation Rate from 55% to 70%
- KR2: Time-to-first-grade from 120 min to 30 min
- KR3: First-week NPS from 32 to 45

**Initiatives:**
- Streamlined onboarding flow (moves KR1, KR2)
- Template-first assignment creation (moves KR2)
- Day-3 check-in email sequence (moves KR1, KR3)

---

### O2: Turn trial users into paying customers
**Why:** Trial conversion at 12% is below target. Each 1% = ~$5K MRR.

- KR1: Trial-to-paid conversion from 12% to 18%
- KR2: Time-to-upgrade from 11 days to 7 days
- KR3: Trial extension requests from 8% to 3%

**Initiatives:**
- Value demonstration in first 48 hours (moves KR1, KR2)
- Premium feature previews during trial (moves KR1)
- Proactive upgrade prompts (moves KR2)

---

### O3: Build foundation for enterprise scale
**Why:** Enterprise pipeline is growing but we're blocked on SSO/compliance.

- KR1: Ship SAML SSO with 2 pilot districts
- KR2: Pass SOC 2 Type 1 audit
- KR3: Support 500+ students per school (10x current)

**Initiatives:**
- SSO implementation (moves KR1)
- Security/compliance sprint (moves KR2)
- Performance optimization (moves KR3)
```
