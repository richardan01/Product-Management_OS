# TA Workload Tracker - Project Brief

## Overview

Building a dashboard for professors to visualize and manage TA workload distribution across their courses. Part of the broader **TA Excellence** initiative at GradeFlow.

## Problem Statement

Professors currently have no visibility into how work is distributed among their TAs. This leads to:
- Some TAs being overloaded while others are underutilized
- Burnout and retention issues with high-performing TAs
- Inconsistent grading turnaround times
- Difficulty planning TA assignments for upcoming semesters

## Opportunity

Our research shows 78% of professors want better tools to manage their TA teams. Current workarounds include spreadsheets, Slack check-ins, and "gut feel" - none of which scale.

## Project Scope

**In Scope:**
- Real-time dashboard showing grading volume per TA
- Historical workload trends (weekly/monthly views)
- Alerts when workload imbalance exceeds threshold
- Export capabilities for department reporting

**Out of Scope (for v1):**
- Automated workload rebalancing
- TA self-service views (planned for v2)
- Integration with external LMS systems beyond Canvas
