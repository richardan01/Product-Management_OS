Based on previous meetings, likely questions:

1. **"Why is GRR slightly down?"**
   - Answer: 3 specific churns due to budget cuts (named accounts). Not product-related. Expect recovery in Q1.

2. **"How does TA Excellence connect to revenue?"**
   - Answer:
     - Enterprise blockers removed (TA permissions shipped)
     - Pipeline influenced: $450K ARR in late-stage deals cite TA features
     - Expansion opportunity: Department-level upsell

3. **"What's the competitive moat?"**
   - Answer:
     - First to market with TA workload visibility
     - AI grading is 2+ generations ahead of Gradescope
     - Switching cost increases with each semester of data

4. **"Are we spending too much on TA features?"**
   - Answer:
     - 2 engineers dedicated (10% of eng org)
     - High ROI: unblocks enterprise deals worth 20x investment
     - Creates platform for future expansion revenue
