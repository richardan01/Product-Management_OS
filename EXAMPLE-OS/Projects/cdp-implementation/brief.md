## The Opportunity

### Problem Statement

The team operates with siloed customer data across multiple tools. There is no single view of the customer, making it impossible to:

- Run coordinated, data-driven lifecycle campaigns
- Build accurate first-party audiences for paid ads
- Understand what drives activation and retention
- Personalize marketing messages by segment

As a result, marketing is less efficient and the team makes decisions based on incomplete data.

## Risks & Mitigations
