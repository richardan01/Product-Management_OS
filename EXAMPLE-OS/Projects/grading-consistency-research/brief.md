# Grading Consistency Research - Project Brief

## Overview

Discovery research to understand the grading consistency problem across TAs. This is exploratory work to inform a potential feature in H2 2025. Part of the broader **TA Excellence** initiative.

## Problem Hypothesis

When multiple TAs grade the same assignment type, there's significant variance in how they apply rubrics. This leads to:
- Student complaints about "unfair" grades
- Professor time spent adjudicating disputes
- Inconsistent learning feedback across sections

**We need to validate this hypothesis and understand the scope of the problem.**

## Research Questions

1. **How big is the problem?** What's the typical grade variance between TAs on similar submissions?

2. **What causes inconsistency?** Is it rubric ambiguity, TA training, subjective interpretation, or something else?

3. **How do professors address it today?** What are the current workarounds?

4. **What would help?** What tools or features would reduce inconsistency?

5. **Is this a buying factor?** Would solving this influence purchase/expansion decisions?
