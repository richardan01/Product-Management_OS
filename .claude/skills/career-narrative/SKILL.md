# Career Narrative

**Agent:** Bruce Wayne (owns the master narrative) + Nightwing (surface-specific compressions) + Gordon (networking message drafts).

## The master narrative structure

Build your master narrative as a 3–4 sentence paragraph that answers: who are you, what have you built/done that's distinctive, what are you building now, what do you want next.

Template:
> [YOUR_NAME] — [YOUR_ROLE] with [X] years across [distinctive domain 1], [distinctive domain 2], and [distinctive domain 3]. Built and [key accomplishment] in [context that signals relevant experience]. Now building [FLAGSHIP PROJECT]: [one-sentence description]. Target: [YOUR_TARGET_ROLE] at [TARGET_COMPANIES] by [YOUR_TARGET_DATE].

**This is the canonical version. All surface compressions derive from it. Nightwing owns the edits.**

## The three-pillar composition

Build three signal pillars from your work history. For each, describe: what it is, and why it matters to a frontier-lab hiring manager.

| Pillar | What it is | Why it matters to a frontier lab |
|---|---|---|
| [FLAGSHIP PROJECT] | [description] | [what it proves] |
| [Most distinctive past role] | [description] | [what it proves] |
| [Third signal — data, research, or novel tech] | [description] | [what it proves] |

Compose the three pillars based on the audience. Reorder by what's most relevant to the specific lab's priorities.

## Surface-specific compressions

**8-word LinkedIn headline:**
> [YOUR_ROLE] building [flagship topic] for [audience]

**30-second verbal (networking)**
> "I'm a PM who spent [time] building [distinctive thing] in [context]. I'm now building [FLAGSHIP PROJECT], [one-sentence description], and looking to move into [YOUR_TARGET_ROLE] at [TARGET_COMPANIES]. Happy to show you [specific artifact]."

**200-word essay bio**
> [YOUR_NAME] is a product manager who has spent [X] years at the intersection of [domain 1] and [domain 2] — most recently as [role] at [Company] ([what you built there]) and [role] at [Company] ([what you built there]). [He/She/They] is currently building [FLAGSHIP PROJECT], [description of what it does and why it matters]. [He/She/They] writes about [topics]. [He/She/They] is based in [location] and targeting [YOUR_TARGET_ROLE] roles at frontier labs by [YOUR_TARGET_DATE].

## Anti-patterns (kill on sight)

- "[Location]-based PM with [X]+ years of experience" — leads with location and tenure, not signal
- "Passionate about AI" — assumed, not differentiating
- "I have a background in…" — passive and forgettable
- Opening with your current title instead of your distinctive claim
