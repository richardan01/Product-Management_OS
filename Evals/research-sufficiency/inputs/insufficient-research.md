# Research summary — Should we build a loyalty/rewards program? (2026)

**Author:** Research Analyst · **Status:** For decision input · **Last updated:** 2026-05-30
**Intended use:** Input to the H2 roadmap decision — fund a merchant loyalty/rewards program?
**Research brief questions:** (1) Do our merchants want loyalty tooling? (2) What is the market opportunity? (3) What would it take to win?

## Findings

- Merchants want loyalty tooling — it is clearly a top unmet need and a major growth lever for us.
- The loyalty-software market is large and fast-growing, so the opportunity is substantial.
- Building a rewards program would meaningfully lift merchant retention and transaction volume.
- We can win because our payments integration is a natural advantage over standalone loyalty apps.

## Market opportunity
- The global loyalty-management market was valued at a high figure and is growing quickly per an industry report.

## Recommendation
- We should fund a full loyalty/rewards program build in H2. This is a clear, high-confidence bet and the single best use of the retention budget — we should commit the roadmap to it now.

- Vendor whitepaper: "The State of Loyalty 2023" (LoyaltyCo marketing report, 2023).
