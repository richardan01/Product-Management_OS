## Key Learnings

1. **Mobile matters more than we thought.** The 29% increase in mobile sessions after our UX improvements suggests we were leaving a lot on the table.

2. **TAs fear AI replacement.** Need to address this head-on in GA messaging. "Amplifier not replacement" framing resonated in beta.

3. **Prof. Williams is a squeaky wheel but not representative.** His scope requests don't match what other professors want. Trust the broader research.

4. **Consistency alerts work better than expected.** 30% self-correction rate is promising. Should invest more here.
